## Supported Bridges & Liquidity

Trails routes transactions through the optimal liquidity sources:

| Provider | Type | Description |
|----------|------|-------------|
| **Relay** | Bridge/Filler | Fast cross-chain execution with instant finality |
| **LiFi** | Aggregator | Multi-bridge aggregation for optimal routes |
| **CCTP V2** | Bridge | Native USDC cross-chain transfers via Circle |

<Note>
Additional bridges and liquidity sources can be integrated on request. Contact us if you need a specific provider.
</Note>

## Chain-Specific Notes

### Low Fee Chains
- **Base** - Lowest fees, recommended for small payments
- **Arbitrum** - Low fees, high liquidity
- **Optimism** - Low fees, Superchain ecosystem

### High Liquidity Chains
- **Ethereum** - Highest liquidity, higher fees
- **Polygon** - Good liquidity, very low fees
- **BNB Chain** - High volume, low fees

### Gaming & NFT Chains
- **Xai** - Gaming-focused L3
- **Ape Chain** - ApeCoin ecosystem
- **B3** - Gaming-focused L2
- **Katana** - Gaming L2 by Ronin
